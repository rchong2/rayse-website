Problem

Due to economic and financial reasons, there has always been a gap in education between privileged and underprivileged children. Recently the gap widened due to a lack of access to computing resources by underprivileged children.

done_all

Solution

To bridge this gap we have developed the solution to establish Academies in underprivileged areas. These Academies are connected to schools and the underprivileged children from these schools attend the Academy during and after school.

trending_up

Method

The Academy is provisioned with at least 20 laptop computers, per our specifications, Internet access and a coach (teacher) that is hired and trained by us. The curriculum and teaching methodology is provided by us.
